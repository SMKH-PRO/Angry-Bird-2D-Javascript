Instructions

Goal is to shoot the bird towards the blocks and once all blocks are shot out of screen you'll win but remember there's a time limit of 1 minute

Press "r" to reset the bird to take another slingshot

Press left or right arrow keys to move the propeller, keep pressing the key will fasten the speed

By pressing "b" you can create birds and so if you create birds around the moving propeller it will shoot the bird.
